# Zanzibar Registration and Copyright Pack
Date verified: April 25, 2026
Project: Hasnet PrintHub

## 1) Why This Document
This pack gives you a practical, submission-ready checklist for registering the business/project and protecting IP in Zanzibar, including what to prepare when authorities request source code and digital assets.

## 2) Authority Split (Important)
1. BPRA handles business/entity registration and industrial property processes in Zanzibar.
2. BPRA IP pages cover trademarks, patents/utility models, industrial designs, and geographical indications.
3. Copyright in Zanzibar is administered by COSOZA (not BPRA service pages).

Practical interpretation for Hasnet PrintHub:
1. Use BPRA for legal entity + business/trademark/IP filings.
2. Use COSOZA for copyright record/evidence process for software and creative works.

## 4) BPRA: Company Registration (Core Legal Setup)
From BPRA Company Registration service page, current baseline requirements include:
1. Applicant NIDA number.
2. Each shareholder NIDA number.
3. Foreign nationals: NIDA or passport and TIN for each director.
4. Attachments listed by BPRA page:
   - Company Constitution,
   - Integrity Form,
   - Consolidated Form.
5. Process runs through ORS online portal.

Recommended for Hasnet:
1. Register as a company first (preferred for scaling/investor onboarding).
2. Use business name registration only if you need an interim sole/partnership step.

## 5) BPRA: Business Name Registration (If Needed)
From BPRA Business Name Registration page:
1. Applicant must be Tanzanian citizen.
2. Age 18+.
3. Valid ID (Tanzanian/Zanzibari resident ID).
4. Personally registered mobile number and personal email.
5. Business physical address and owner residential address.
6. Registration types: individual or partnership.

## 6) BPRA: Industrial Property for Hasnet PrintHub
For software businesses, practical BPRA IP protection usually starts with trademark registration for brand assets.

### 5.1 Trademark (Most Immediate)
BPRA IP page lists required items:
1. Form No. 13.
2. Applicant details.
3. Agent details and Form No. 25 if applicable.
4. Three reproductions of mark.
5. List of goods/services by Nice Classification.
6. Priority documents if applicable.
7. Signed form.

BPRA page fee references currently list:
1. Application: Sh. 150,000/-
2. Publication: Sh. 30,000/-
3. Registration: Sh. 100,000/-
4. Renewal: Sh. 80,000/-

### 5.2 Patent/Utility Model (Only If You Have Patentable Novelty)
BPRA IP page lists:
1. Form No. 1.
2. Invention title.
3. Applicant/inventor details.
4. Detailed specification (abstract, claims, drawings).
5. Priority docs if any.
6. Payment receipt for application/advertisement.
7. Maintenance fee per year up to 20 years.

### 5.3 Industrial Design (If Filing Kiosk Industrial Form)
BPRA IP page lists:
1. Form No. 8.
2. Applicant/creator details.
3. Product information.
4. Specimens or drawings.
5. Priority docs if any.
6. Payment receipt for application/advertisement.

## 7) Copyright Route for Software and Creative Assets
1. BPRA pages do not publish software copyright registration requirements.
2. COSOZA is the Zanzibar copyright office.
3. COSOZA states copyright exists automatically once expressed in tangible form, and registered works help as evidence in infringement matters.

What to do in practice:
1. Contact COSOZA and request their latest prescribed registration forms and fee schedule for software/computer programs and artistic/branding assets.
2. Submit software and creative asset evidence pack (section 7 below).
3. Keep stamped receipts, application references, and issued certificates in your legal vault.

## 8) Source Code and Asset Submission Pack (Ready for BPRA/COSOZA/Legal)
Prepare one clean package per release:

1. Ownership and identity:
   - Company certificate / business registration certificate.
   - Director/shareholder IDs and TIN where required.
   - Authorization letter if filing through an agent.
2. Software evidence:
   - Source snapshot zip (`hasnet-printhub-src-YYYYMMDD.zip`).
   - Commit hash manifest (`git rev-parse HEAD`, branch, tag).
   - Build/run instructions (`README`, deployment script notes).
   - Architecture summary (backend, edge-agent, admin/customer apps).
3. Product assets:
   - Logos and branding files (source + exports).
   - UI mockups (`mockup/1.jpeg` to `mockup/4.jpeg`).
   - Product screenshots and customer flow diagrams.
4. Legal declarations:
   - Declaration of authorship/ownership.
   - Third-party component list and licenses.
   - Assignment agreements from contributors (if any).
5. Metadata:
   - Work title/version.
   - First publication/use date.
   - Contact details and filing representative.
6. Proof integrity:
   - SHA-256 hashes for each submitted file.
   - Printed manifest signed and dated.

## 9) Recommended Filing Order
1. Company registration at BPRA (if not yet finalized).
2. Business/trade name and trademark filing (Form 13 + mark specimens).
3. Copyright filing workflow with COSOZA for software and creative works.
4. Optional patent/industrial design only if strategic and novel enough.

## 10) Filing Execution Checklist
1. Open/verify ORS account (`orsz.bpra.go.tz`).
2. Complete online application and upload attachments.
3. Pay via BPRA accepted channels and store payment references.
4. Track status and respond quickly to registrar queries.
5. Archive all final certificates and official correspondence.
